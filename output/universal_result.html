EDITORIAL

An Odyssey of Depth and Design

Omega's celebrated Planet Ocean is reborn, emerging with a refined silhouette and a modern architectural spirit.

When Omega first unveiled the Planet Ocean, the ambition was to venture deeper, both in philosophy and in the physical world of underwater exploration. The original, introduced two decades ago, possessed an unmistakable aquatic soul, paying homage to the iconic Seamaster 300 designs of the mid-century while introducing technical advancements that doubled the water resistance of its forebear....

An icon reborn, blending technical depth with grace.

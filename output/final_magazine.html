A Duet of Grit and Glamour

Beyond its celebrated sneakers, the Venetian house unveils a clothing collection imbued with the poetic spirit of the 1970s.

From the artisanal heart of Venice, Golden Goose has ascended over a quarter-century into a global phenomenon. This meteoric rise is largely attributed to its iconic, artfully weathered sneakers and a steadfast commitment to the tangible art of the brick-and-mortar experience. Yet, amidst this well-deserved acclaim, a quieter, equally compelling narrative often remains unsung: the sheer elegance of its apparel.
